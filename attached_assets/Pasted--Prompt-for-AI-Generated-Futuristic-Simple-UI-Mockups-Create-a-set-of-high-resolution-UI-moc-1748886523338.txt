## 5. Visual & Interaction Guidelines

1. *Futuristic Simplicity:*

   * Overall feel: minimalistic, lots of whitespace, geometric shapes, subtle neon-blue accents (#1907FF) to evoke a high-tech automotive vibe.
   * Icons: simple line icons (tyres, vehicles, wrenches, stars, maps) in black (#000000) or white (#FFFFFF) with occasional blue (#1907FF) fills on hover.

2. *Consistent Spacing & Alignment:*

   * Use multiples of 8 px for all padding/margin (8 px, 16 px, 24 px, 32 px).
   * Align form labels above inputs, left-aligned text.
   * Ensure each card has consistent 8 px radius and shadow (RGBA(0,0,0,0.05)).

3. *Focus & Hover States:*

   * *Buttons:* default blue (#1907FF) → hover darker (#1306CC); outline buttons (#1907FF border/#FFFFFF fill) → hover fill blue (#1907FF)/text white (#FFFFFF).
   * *Inputs/Dropdowns:* focus outline in #1907FF.
   * *Cards:* on hover, border turns #1907FF and slight lift (translateY(–4 px)).

4. *Typography Hierarchy:*

   * *Orbitron (or similar) for all headings/titles:* bold, crisp edges, spaced out letters to feel futuristic.
   * *Roboto (or Open Sans) for all body text/form labels:* clean, easy to read.
   * Maintain consistent line-heights: headings 1.2×, body text 1.5×.

5. *Color Usage:*

   * *Black (#000000):* primary text, header background, footer elements, icons.
   * *White (#FFFFFF):* backgrounds for cards, forms, modals, hero overlays.
   * *Electric Blue (#1907FF):* CTAs (buttons, active tabs, highlights, link hovers), icon accents, focus outlines, star ratings.
   * *Light Gray (#F5F5F5) & Medium Gray (#CCCCCC):* background stripes, input borders, disabled states, subtext.
   * *Red (#D9534F) & Green (#28A745):* minimal usage for “Clear Filters” link, “Out of Stock” badges (red), “In Stock” badges (green).
